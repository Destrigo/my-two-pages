Privacy Policy for HeBe Order
Effective Date: December 19, 2025

Introduction
HeBe Order ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application handles information.

Information We Collect
HeBe Order is designed to work entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our servers.

Data Stored Locally
The app stores the following information only on your device:

Login credentials (stored in device's local storage)
Form data temporarily during order creation
User preferences
This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly submit an order via email.

Email Functionality
When you submit an order, the app uses EmailJS service to send the order details to ordini@newcossrl.it. The information sent includes:

Agent name
Customer information
Order details
Notes
This email is sent directly from your device to the recipient. We do not store or process this information on any servers.

Third-Party Services
EmailJS
We use EmailJS to facilitate email sending. EmailJS may process email data according to their own privacy policy: https://www.emailjs.com/legal/privacy-policy/

Data Security
All data is stored locally on your device using industry-standard encryption provided by your operating system (iOS or Android). You are responsible for protecting access to your device.

Children's Privacy
This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be posted within the app and will be effective immediately upon posting.

Data Deletion
To delete all data:

Uninstall the app from your device
All locally stored data will be automatically removed
Contact
For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact:

Email: ordini@newcossrl.it
Company: HeBe / New Cos S.r.l.
Your Rights
Since no data is collected by us, there is no personal data to access, correct, or delete from our systems. All data resides solely on your device.

Compliance
This app is compliant with:

GDPR (EU General Data Protection Regulation)
Apple App Store Review Guidelines
Google Play Store Policies
No Data Collection Statement: HeBe Order does not collect, transmit, or store any user data on external servers. All functionality is performed locally on your device.
